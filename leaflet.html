About this Leaflet map

This map loads bike path data directly from the SBCTA (San Bernardino County Transportation Authority) ArcGIS REST service. Segments are symbolized by facility class (I, II, III). A second layer is from San Bernardino County School Sites (Authoriative) as red dots. The layer controls and a legend allow you to compare bikeway coverage with school sites.
